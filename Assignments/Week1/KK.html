ABOUT KVK

KVK is one of thee largest applied science universities in lithunia. It is located in the beautiful port city of klaipeda .

The university offers 6 faculties and 16 study programs in the fields of engineering, business, health sciences, and social sciences.

The university has a diverse student body, with students from all over Lithuania and many other countries. The university also has a strong international focus, with nany opportunities for students to study abroad and participate in international research projects.

overall, KVK is a great chioce for student who are looking for a high-quality education in a beautiful and vibrant city.
